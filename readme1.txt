MATCH IT! - GAME DIRECTIONS
-------------------------
Match It! is a game where the player must match the pieces of the puzzle to recreate the full image of the Pokemon. 

It is a simple drag and drop game. Use your mouse to drag each piece of the puzzle. 

No issues currently.

May expand with more Pokemon to recreate.
